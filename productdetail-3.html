Birthday Gift for my little buddy

We like it very much but for one issue. The material choice for the inside that comes into contact with the dog's fur, while soft, makes it incredibly difficult to remove the fur that sticks to it. Our one dog sheds quite a bit and he wears the harness much of the day as he has laryngeal paralysis and cannot wear a collar, hence a huge amount of fur stuck to the inside of the harness. I think some kind of heavy duty cotton material would have worked better. I especially like the front loop to attach a leash as this helps with the same issue.
