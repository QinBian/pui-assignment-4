Product Description

Create an eye-catching, stylish look for your pup, while also keeping him safe and secure with the Chai’s Choice 3M Reflective Dog Harness. Lovingly crafted to remove pressure from your dog’s neck and disperse it through cushy and lightweight mesh padding on the chest and belly, this harness also has reflective piping interwoven into its durable nylon exterior to make sure he stays visible while you’re walking him at night. Adjustable straps and a wide variety of color and size choices make for a completely customizable experience. And you can say goodbye to chaotic walks and car rides, simply by attaching his leash to the chest-mounted O-ring to discourage pulling, or buckling him in for road trips by slipping a seatbelt through the sturdy built-in handle on top of the harness.

I ordered this harness after a failed attempt on another brand which didn't fit my dog very well.
